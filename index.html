DriveVerse

Exploring the World of Car

DriveVerse is an interactive digital experience designed to immerse users in the captivating world of automobiles. Developed using Photoshop and Adobe XD, this project combines sleek UI design with engaging content to educate and excite users about high-performance cars, engine types, automotive history, and legendary manufacturers. The interface includes: Start Page Navigation: A visual dashboard guiding users to key categories like Cars, Engine Types, Car History, and Fun Facts. Car Profiles: Detailed pages on iconic models like the Porsche 911 GT3 RS, BMW M8, Mercedes SLS AMG, Audi R8, and Ferrari 812 Superfast, featuring technical specs, design highlights, and brand identity. Engine Explorer: An intuitive breakdown of Combustion, Hybrid, and Electric engines with visual icons and descriptions.



Breaking Limits

” – Nike Ad Composite Campaign by Aaditya Mittal

This ad composite campaign, titled “Breaking Limits,” is a multi-platform creative initiative developed to promote Nike Running Shoes. Conceptualized and designed by Aaditya Mittal, the campaign focuses on empowering athletes—both amateur and professional—to transcend physical and mental boundaries using Nike’s high-performance footwear. Blending inspirational storytelling with bold visuals, the campaign sets itself apart from competitors like Adidas, Puma, and Reebok by diving deeper into personal endurance, emotional resilience, and real-world athletic struggles. From print layouts for Runner's World magazine to dynamic social media visuals tailored for Instagram, the campaign ensures maximum engagement across platforms. Highlights include: Motivational storytelling vs generic brand slogans. High-energy action photography and dynamic sketches. Strategic media placement and emphasis on emotional connection. After-effects showcasing increased brand awareness, social media interaction, and potential sales. This project doesn’t just sell shoes—it sells the mindset of pushing beyond limits.
